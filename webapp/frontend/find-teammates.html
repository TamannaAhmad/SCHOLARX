How Matching Works

Your matches are calculated based on the following criteria:

  • Overall Match: Combined score of all matching factors
  • Skill Match: How well the user's skills match the project requirements
  • Availability Match: How well your available times overlap with the user's schedule

Scores range from 0% (no match) to 100% (perfect match).
